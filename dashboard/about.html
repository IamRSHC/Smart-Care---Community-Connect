The Problem

Old age homes run on manual rounds and no digital record.

Low staff-to-resident ratios mean delayed response to falls, abnormal vitals, and prolonged inactivity — with no continuous record of a resident's health or activity between checks. SmartCare closes that gap with low-cost sensors, rule-based anomaly detection, and a live caregiver dashboard.
